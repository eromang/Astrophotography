# External Resources

Curated index of external astrophotography resources — YouTube channels, websites, tools, vendor docs, and communities. Seeded from links and clippings already present in the vault; extend it freely.

**How Claude uses this page:** recommendations are **topic-driven** (no last-reviewed dates). When we work on a target, technique, or piece of gear, Claude matches the **Sujets / tags** column and suggests the relevant resource(s) to (re)watch or read.

> [!note] Maintenance
> One row per resource. Keep **Sujets / tags** as lowercase keywords (e.g. `redcat, pixinsight, narrowband, guiding`) so topic-matching works. **Pourquoi utile** is a free note — what it taught you or why to keep it.
