## Notes

- Also known as the Swan, Horseshoe, Checkmark or Lobster Nebula (NGC 6618)
- One of the brightest and most massive star-forming HII regions in the sky — bright core vs fainter outer nebulosity (HDR candidate)
- Strong Ha and OIII emission — ideal for [[Antlia-FQuad]]
- Summer target (RA 18h 20m, Dec -16deg 11') — visible June through August from Tuntange, same window as [[M16-Eagle]]
- Max transit altitude ~24deg from latitude 49.7degN — always at high airmass (~2.3), ~2deg lower than M16
- Pair with [[M16-Eagle]] in a single [[RedCat-51]] frame: 2.45deg apart (M17 is SSE of M16), long-axis sky PA 169deg — see [[M16-Campaign-2026]]
- Use [[QuadBand-OSC-Workflow]] (+ [[HDR-Workflow]] for the bright core)
