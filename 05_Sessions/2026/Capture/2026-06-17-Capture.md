> [!tip] ASIAIR GoTo: **18h 19.8m / −15° 00′ @ PA 169°**
> Point at the **midpoint**, not M16 — centring on M16 clips M17's edge (2.45° sep vs 2.69° half-frame). Use the **Framing & Mosaic** tool: search M16, recentre to these coordinates, set Image Rotation 169°, GoTo. Plate-solve and confirm centre ≈ 18h 19.8m / −15° and angle ≈ 169° before starting.
