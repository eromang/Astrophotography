## Calibration Plan (if doing darks)

1. Cap the camera completely (no light leaks)
2. Set ASI2600MC Pro to **-20°C**, wait 5 min for stable cooling
3. Configure ASIAIR for 300s, Gain 100, dark mode
4. Capture **25 frames** of 300s darks
5. Wait for cooling stability between sequences
6. Optionally continue with 180s (5 more frames to complete the existing set)
