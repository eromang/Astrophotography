# COOWOO Dew Heater Strip

USB-powered dew heater strip to prevent frost, condensation and fogging on the [[RedCat-51]] objective lens during imaging sessions.

---

## Specifications

| Specification | Value |
|---------------|-------|
| Length | 43 cm |
| Width | 4.5 cm |
| Material | Polyester and cotton |
| Voltage | 5V USB |
| Current | 1.4A |
| Power | 7W |
| USB cable length | 1.5m |
| Controller cable length | 80 cm |
| Heat-up time | ~10 min to reach 40–60°C |
| Temperature modes | Low, Medium, High |

---

## Configuration

Wraps around the [[RedCat-51]] dew shield (80mm diameter, 255mm circumference). The 43 cm length is sufficient to wrap around with overlap.

**Power source:** USB from [[Jackery-Explorer-500]] or [[ASIAIR]] USB port.

**Controller:** Inline temperature controller with 3 modes — use Low for most nights, Medium/High for heavy dew or frost conditions.

---

## When to Use

| Condition | Setting | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Humidity > 75% | Low | Prevent gradual dew formation |
| Humidity > 85% | Medium | Active dew conditions |
| Frost / near freezing | High | Prevent ice on lens |
| Dry night (< 65% humidity) | Off | Not needed |

> The [[TS-Dew-Shield-Cap]] provides passive dew protection. Use the heater strip in addition when humidity is high or temperatures drop significantly during the session.
